Don't be bussy, be attractive

The long-term goals of Project Yachi Yachi include creating a sustainable platform for youth engagement, fostering a strong network of young change-makers, and expanding the festival's reach to inspire youth worldwide. Ultimately, the project aims to contribute to a more sustainable and inclusive future by empowering youth to be active agents of positive change.

“ "Yachi Yachi is a transformative initiative focused on empowering and mobilizing youth to drive sustainable change within their communities and beyond. The project aims to provide young individuals with the necessary tools, resources, and opportunities to become active agents of positive social, economic, and environmental transformation. ”
